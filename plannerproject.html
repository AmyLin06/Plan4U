It's all in the details

"Title" is a website that allows users to plan out their weeks so that they don't feel the overwhelming pressure of a busy life. Title" has 3 major functions: the weekly planner, the to-do and reminder list, and the money tracker. Using these in the long term, people can stay on track and work effectively towards their goal. Like the french writer, Antoine de Saint-Exupéry, once said, "A goal without a plan is just a wish."
